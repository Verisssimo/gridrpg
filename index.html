Secret File: Disappearance of Dr. Hartmann and Transport Vehicle

Data:June 15, 1819

Status:CONFIDENTIAL

Involved Parties:Dra. Evelyn Carten and Dr. Artur Miles.

Description:We need immediate attention for a critical situation. Dr. Victor Hartmann has been missing since last night, along with the transport vehicle designated for Project Nimbus. He left the research unit around 10:30 PM, claiming he was taking materials for analysis.

Attempts to communicate have failed, and the vehicle cannot be tracked due to the sensitivity of the project. Instruct the security team to initiate a discreet investigation, but the priority is to recover the vehicle and ensure that any documentation does not fall into the wrong hands. Commander Ward is already mobilizing a team to cover potential leaks and information breaches.

We cannot rule out any external involvement, including the Consortium or other parties interested in our advancements. National security is at risk.
